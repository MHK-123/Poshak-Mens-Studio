The Process

Bespoke Tailoring

An intimate journey from concept to creation. We construct garments that are uniquely yours, meticulously crafted over 50 hours of handiwork.

01

Consultation

Your vision discussed over whiskey or espresso. Fabric selection from world-renowned mills and taking precise singular measurements.

02

The Baste Fitting

A "skeleton" of the suit is assembled. This un-finished garment is fitted to your body to ensure the perfect drape and silhouette.

03

Final Commission

The final hand-stitching, buttonholes, and pressing. You wear the finished garment home, ready to command any room.
